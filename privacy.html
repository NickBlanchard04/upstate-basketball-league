Website privacy

Clear choices, limited data, and no advertising profiles.

UBL

The short version

UBL uses essential website services to publish league information. Optional analytics stays off until you allow it. We do not sell visitor information or use analytics for advertising.

Essential website activity

The website requests public schedules, scores, standings, team information, and approved gallery images from UBL-managed files and Google services. These requests are necessary to show current league information and do not depend on analytics consent.

Optional analytics

After you choose “Allow analytics,” the site may use Google Analytics and the UBL's private performance feed. The UBL feed records only:

  • The page viewed and referring website host.
  • General device class and browser viewport size.
  • Page load time, largest-contentful-paint time, and layout-shift measurement.

The UBL feed does not include names, email addresses, form entries, advertising identifiers, or precise location. Google Analytics may place first-party analytics cookies after consent. Advertising storage, Google signals, and ad personalization are disabled in the UBL configuration.

How your choice works

Your browser stores “granted” or “denied” under ubl-analytics-consent-v1 in local storage. This preference is not used for advertising. Declining prevents optional analytics from loading on future visits from that browser. Clearing browser storage will ask you again.

Scores, photographs, and communications

Game results and team information are league records supplied by authorized representatives. Gallery photographs are published only through the UBL approval workflow and may include student-athletes, coaches, officials, or spectators. Email links open your email provider; the public website does not collect the message itself.

Questions or removal requests

To ask about website data, correct public league information, or request review of a photograph, email info.upstatebasketballleague@gmail.com.
