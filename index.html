League standards

Clear expectations protect the game, the players, and every program involved.

Mission & purpose

Provide smaller programs with structured competition, sportsmanship, and a meaningful championship experience rooted in faith.

Communication policy

Schedule changes, disputes, and league decisions move through each team’s designated representative and the commissioner.

Sportsmanship & discipline

Players, coaches, and spectators are expected to honor opponents and officials. Repeated or serious issues move through a documented review process.

Eligibility & cancellations

Teams must verify player eligibility before competition and communicate weather or facility cancellations as early as possible.

Referee expectations

Officials should receive confirmed game details in advance and have authority to enforce game and sportsmanship standards.

Questions about the league?

Coaches and team representatives can contact league leadership for scheduling, rules, and officiating.
