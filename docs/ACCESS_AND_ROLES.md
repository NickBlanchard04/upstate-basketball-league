# UBL Access And Roles

The private `Access Roster` tab in the [UBL League Control Panel - 2026-27](https://docs.google.com/spreadsheets/d/1AVo5oRxSCFuTrkCK4MA30vT5u_6XuLAXIvyaqnWgcRE/edit) is the source of truth for names, verified Google accounts, access levels, and onboarding status. Do not copy private account details into this public repository.

## Access levels

- **System owner:** Owns Apps Script triggers, the coach portal, public feeds, backups, and access changes.
- **Commissioner:** Edits the control panel, manages schedule exceptions, corrects published scores, and completes weekly review.
- **Coach score portal editor:** Can enter final scores only. This role cannot see the private control panel or audit sheet.
- **Public visitor:** Can view the website and public feeds only.

## Least-access rules

1. Give each program one primary score submitter unless the commissioner approves a backup.
2. Add only a verified Google account supplied directly by the representative.
3. Never share the control panel with team representatives.
4. Review the private roster before every season and remove departed representatives promptly.
5. Record the date each account was verified or removed.
6. Do not send passwords, recovery codes, or owner-account credentials by email.

The roster currently tracks the commissioner, The King's School, Wilton Baptist, Perth, HV Rocks, HV Flames, and the system owner. Pending representatives remain without access until their Google account is verified.
