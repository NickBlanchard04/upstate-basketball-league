# Team Representative Score Guide

Use only the private portal sent for your program. Do not share the link publicly.

## Submit a final

1. Open your team portal after the game is final.
2. Confirm the date, division, away team, and home team.
3. Enter both final scores in the yellow cells.
4. Check `Submit` once.
5. Read `Status` before closing the sheet.

The system records the rostered representative's name. When reporting ownership is activated, the home team's designated reporter submits the final; the other team verifies the result and requests a correction if needed.

## Status messages

- `Published to website`: results, standings, homepage information, and playoff seeding were updated.
- `Review ...`: verify the unusual result, then check `Submit` again if it is correct.
- `Rejected`: fix the issue named in the status and resubmit.
- `Pilot complete - private`: the practice result was recorded without changing the public site.

## Request a correction

Open `Correction Request`, choose the completed game, enter both corrected scores and a short reason, then check `Submit`. The result changes only after commissioner approval.

Do not put discipline, medical, injury, or eligibility information in the portal. Send operational questions to [info.upstatebasketballleague@gmail.com](mailto:info.upstatebasketballleague@gmail.com).
