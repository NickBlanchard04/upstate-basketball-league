League standards

Clear expectations protect the game, the players, and every program involved.

Faith · Competition · Character

Faith. Competition. Kingdom impact.

The Upstate Basketball League exists to honor God through competition, build character, and unite our communities on and off the court.

Mission & purpose

Provide smaller high school programs with structured competition, strong sportsmanship, and a meaningful championship experience rooted in faith.

Communication policy

Schedule changes, disputes, and league decisions move through each team’s designated representative and the commissioner.

Sportsmanship & discipline

Coaches, players, and spectators represent their programs and the league with respect. Discipline issues follow a documented league review process.

Eligibility policy

Each program confirms player eligibility before competition and addresses questions through league leadership before game day.

Cancellation policy

Weather and emergency cancellations are communicated promptly, with both programs working through league leadership to reschedule.

Referee expectations

Officials are supported through clear communication, consistent standards, and respectful conduct from every program.
