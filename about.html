About UBL

A league built to give growing basketball programs meaningful competition and a championship worth pursuing.

Faith · Competition · Kingdom impact

This is UBL

UBL is a faith-rooted varsity basketball league built to give smaller programs organized competition, dependable leadership, and a meaningful postseason.

01
Who we are
A structured league that brings faith, competition, sportsmanship, and clear standards onto the same court.
02
Who we serve
Smaller high school programs competing in Boys Varsity and Girls Varsity divisions.
03
Where we play
Across upstate New York, with regular-season games hosted in member-program gyms and a shared postseason championship.

How a UBL season works

The culture behind the commitments

Our commitments only matter when they shape the way we lead, communicate, prepare, and respond. These are the habits we want every UBL program to experience.

01 How we lead chevron_right

Leadership starts with service, keeps the purpose bigger than the final score, and protects the standard for every program.

  • Every athlete, coach, official, and volunteer has value.
  • Team success never comes at the expense of character.
  • Leadership is an opportunity to serve.
02 How we interact chevron_right

Trust grows through direct, respectful, and consistent communication before, during, and after competition.

  • Coaches model composure and address conflict with maturity.
  • Officials are treated as essential partners in the game.
  • Programs communicate early, clearly, and honestly.
03 How we work chevron_right

We prepare with care, compete with energy, and keep improving the league one dependable decision at a time.

  • Schedules, standards, and expectations are made clear.
  • Programs arrive prepared to compete with excellence.
  • Each season should leave a stronger foundation for the next.
