About UBL

A league built to give growing basketball programs meaningful competition and a championship worth pursuing.

Faith · Competition · Kingdom impact
Our mission

Faith. Competition. Kingdom impact.

The Upstate Basketball League exists to honor God through competition, build character, and unite our communities on and off the court.

Why UBL exists

Meaningful competition for growing programs.

UBL provides smaller schools, homeschool teams, and growing programs with organized varsity competition, consistent league standards, and a meaningful championship experience.

Commissioner

Andy Walts

Andy has served as an Upward Sports basketball league commissioner since 2022. Through New Hope Community Church in Queensbury, he helps lead a sports ministry serving more than 220 student-athletes with over 50 volunteers, with experience spanning officiating, coaching, administration, marketing, social media, and organization.

Referee Coordinator

Chris Webster

Chris has officiated for 36 years and has helped teach new officials joining the Section 2 Board. His goal is to have experienced officials on every UBL game.
