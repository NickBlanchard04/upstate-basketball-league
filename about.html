What winning means

How We WinMatters

At UBL, winning matters—but the scoreboard tells only part of the story. We compete with excellence, walk in humility, and measure success by the character we display in both victory and defeat.

Compete hard

We give our best because effort becomes worship when it is offered to God.

“Whatever you do, work at it with all your heart, as working for the Lord.”

Colossians 3:23 · NIV®
Walk humbly

Strength and success should always be matched by justice, mercy, and humility.

“To act justly and to love mercy and to walk humbly with your God.”

Micah 6:8 · NIV®
Honor God

Every choice—on the court and away from it—should point beyond ourselves.

“Whatever you do, do it all for the glory of God.”

1 Corinthians 10:31 · NIV®

Our Core Commitments

The standards that shape how we prepare, compete, lead, win, and lose.

  1. 01

    Faith First

    Honor God in how we prepare, compete, lead, win, and lose.

  2. 02

    Compete With Excellence

    Play hard, prepare well, and pursue victory with integrity.

  3. 03

    Character in Every Result

    Respond to both victory and defeat with maturity and humility.

  4. 04

    Clear Communication

    Build trust through dependable, consistent league leadership.

  5. 05

    Build Something Lasting

    Create a foundation that serves athletes and programs for years to come.
