How a UBL season works

League at a glance

Built for growing programs.

UBL is a faith-rooted varsity basketball league offering organized competition, dependable leadership, and a meaningful postseason.

Who we are
A structured league that brings faith, competition, sportsmanship, and clear standards onto the same court.
Who we serve
Smaller high school programs competing in Boys Varsity and Girls Varsity divisions.
Where we play
Across upstate New York, with games hosted in member-program gyms and a shared postseason championship.

What winning means

How We WinMatters

At UBL, winning matters—but the scoreboard tells only part of the story. We compete with excellence, walk in humility, and measure success by the character we display in both victory and defeat.

Compete hard

We give our best because effort becomes worship when it is offered to God.

“Whatever you do, work at it with all your heart, as working for the Lord.”

Colossians 3:23 · NIV
Walk humbly

Strength and success should always be matched by justice, mercy, and humility.

“To act justly and to love mercy and to walk humbly with your God.”

Micah 6:8 · NIV
Honor God

Every choice—on the court and away from it—should point beyond ourselves.

“Whatever you do, do it all for the glory of God.”

1 Corinthians 10:31 · NIV
